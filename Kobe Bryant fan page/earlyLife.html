Early Life of Kobe Bryant


  • Bryant was born on August 23, 1978. in Philadelphia.
  • Son of former NBA player Joe Bryant and Pamela Cox Bryant.
  • He was standing 6'6 ft and weighed 210 lbs.
  • He was named after famous Kobe beef, Japan.
  • Kobe is fluent in Italian, because his family lived there.
  • He went to Lower Merion High School in Ardmore, located in Philadelphia.

  • After graduating high school, Kobe decided to take his basketball talents to NBA in 1996.
  • He was selected by the Charlotte Hornets 13th overall pick.
  • After the draft, Hornets decided to trade him to Lakers.
  • In late '90-s he met his future wife Vanessa and they got married.
  • They have 4 daughters, one daughter (Gigi) died in a helicopter crash with Kobe on January 26, 2020.
