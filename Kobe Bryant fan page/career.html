Kobe's career


Finals record: 5-2. First NBA Finals was in 2000. when Lakers won the championship. In 2001 and 2002 they defended NBA title. In 2004 they were defeated in NBA finals by Detroit Pistons. In 2008 Lakers went to the Finals and got defeated by Boston Celtics. In the next two years Lakers won the title where and Kobe won the Finals MVP award.

On January 22, 2006, Kobe scored the most points against Toronto Raptors in a single game in a newer of NBA history(81 points). Kobe holds second place in the number of points in a single game (first place holds Wilt Chamberlain who scored 100 points in 1962.).
